FAQ

Common Printing Questions

Which file is best for printing?

Print-ready PDF with CMYK colors, 300 DPI images, 3 mm bleed, and outlined fonts is best.

Can you design my brochure or banner?

Yes, graphic design support is included as a service for layout correction, new designs, and print-ready artwork.

Do you handle urgent orders?

Rush orders can be handled depending on quantity, material availability, finishing, and current production queue.

Can I order custom size printing?

Yes, custom printing supports special sizes, unique materials, personalized gifts, labels, packaging, and signage.
